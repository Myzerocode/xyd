# Callouts
<Subtitle>
    Use callouts to add eye-catching context to your content.
</Subtitle>

## Overview
Callouts are used to highlight important information or to provide additional context to your content. They are typically used to draw attention to a specific piece of information or to provide a warning or alert.

## Example
<Callout>
    This is a generic callout message to highlight important information.
</Callout>
